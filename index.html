FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your first representative.

Is this legal? Can someone truly represent me at a formal meeting? +
Yes. Professional representation is a well-established legal practice. StandIn Corp operates under scope-of-authority agreements and, where required, limited power of attorney instruments. Our legal team ensures every engagement is fully compliant with local law.
How do I know my confidential information is safe? +
Every representative signs a comprehensive NDA before any briefing. All platform data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Role-based access ensures reps only see information relevant to their specific active engagement. We maintain full audit trails.
How quickly can a rep be dispatched? +
Standard booking requires 48–72 hours notice for optimal matching. We also offer an Urgent Dispatch service for same-day and 24-hour deployments, subject to availability, at a 40% surcharge on the base engagement fee.
What happens if the rep performs below standard? +
We maintain a satisfaction guarantee. If you are not satisfied with the engagement, our quality team investigates within 24 hours. We offer a partial or full refund depending on the circumstances, backed by our professional indemnity insurance coverage.
Can the rep sign documents or make commitments on my behalf? +
Only within the precise scope defined in your scope-of-authority agreement. For contract signing, a limited power of attorney is prepared. Nothing beyond your defined scope can be agreed to — this is a core safeguard of our service model.
Do you operate outside Nairobi? +
Yes. We currently have verified representatives across 15+ counties in Kenya. For international engagements, we offer cross-border deployment including travel logistics coordination. Contact us for a custom quote for any engagement outside our standard coverage area.
How do I communicate with my rep during the meeting? +
Tier 2 and Tier 3 engagements include secure real-time communication via the StandIn Corp platform. You can send instructions, respond to questions, and receive live updates while your rep is in the meeting room.
How is StandIn Corp different from a staffing agency? +
Fundamentally different. Staffing agencies place people into jobs. We deploy credentialed professionals for single, specific engagements under legal agreements that protect all parties. We are a corporate representation firm — a new category entirely.
